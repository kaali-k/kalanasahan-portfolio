About This Project

K.S.D BIZ Manager is a powerful and all-in-one business management application designed to simplify daily business operations. Built with a modern, user-friendly interface, this app provides a robust suite of tools for managing customers, orders, sales, expenses, and more, all within a single, secure, and private environment. The core principle of this app is to give small business owners complete control over their business data. All data is stored securely and locally on your device in a local SQL database, ensuring your information remains private and accessible at all times, with or without an internet connection.

Key Features:

  • Modular Management System: The app features a highly modular architecture, allowing users to enable or disable specific modules like Customers, Orders, Inventory, Sales Invoices, Purchase Invoices, Expenses, Suppliers, Employees, and Services according to their business needs.
  • Comprehensive Financial Tracking: Track revenue, expenses, and profit in real-time, with the ability to generate detailed reports in PDF format for various modules.
  • Customer & Supplier Management: Maintain a complete database of customers and suppliers, including contact information and transaction history for easy access.
  • Inventory & Service Management: Keep a real-time track of product inventory, including low stock and expiring items. You can also manage a list of services with analytics on total revenue and usage count.
  • Invoicing and Orders: Create, manage, and track Sales Invoices, Purchase Invoices and Orders with different statuses (Pending, Completed, Unpaid).
  • Employee and Payroll Management: Manage employee details, track their status, and handle salary payments with a built-in feature to automatically mark salaries as due.
  • Expense and Recurring Expense Tracking: Record one-time expenses and set up recurring expense rules with automated due date notifications.
  • Dynamic Notifications: A robust notification system provides timely reminders for due orders, recurring expenses, and unpaid purchase invoices.
  • Data Management & Security: Securely back up and restore your business data with local database export/import functionality.
  • Personalized App Experience: Users can customize the app with a system-wide Dark/Light mode and select their preferred currency symbol.

Technology Stack & Development Focus

  • Core Framework: Built entirely on Flutter with a Dart codebase, ensuring a single, high-performance application for both Android and iOS.
  • Local Database: Utilizes sqflite for a robust local database, providing fast, efficient, and offline-first data persistence for all business information.
  • State Management: The app employs a clean and scalable state management architecture, leveraging Provider and ChangeNotifier to handle complex data flows across multiple modules efficiently.
  • App Configuration & User Preferences: shared_preferences is used to manage app-wide settings like theme and currency preferences, while a dynamic, data-driven approach is used for module selection.
  • Dynamic PDF Generation: The project integrates pdf and printing packages to dynamically generate and share detailed PDF reports for various business data, including invoices, orders, expenses, and inventory.
  • Native API Integration: The application uses image_picker to attach receipts to expenses and flutter_local_notifications to provide localized, scheduled notifications for key business events.

Development Focus:

A key development focus was building a modular system that is both powerful and customizable, allowing business owners to tailor the app to their specific needs. The integration of local notifications was a significant challenge, requiring careful handling of scheduled reminders for due dates without relying on an external server. The data-driven PDF reporting system was also a major achievement, demonstrating the ability to transform complex database data into professional, printable documents. This project showcases the ability to build a comprehensive, feature-rich, and reliable business management application.
